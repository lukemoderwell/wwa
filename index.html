Wedding Services

Ceremony

We provide music as your guests are being seated and will continue to play for the processional, special requests, and recessional. As a trio we are the perfect size to create an intimate atmosphere for your ceremony.

Cocktails

As your guests start to mingle with hors d'oeuvres and drinks we will provide the music to set the tone for the rest of the evening. The music will be soft, yet engaging for your guests and let them know you have thought about every detail!

Reception

If you have seen us before you know we love to pull out the tambourine and get people on the dance floor!  As your reception band I will emcee the entire evening and work with your other vendors to make sure the night runs smoothly.  While we will play our “live” set as much as you want, we understand there are some songs that just don’t fit our group…. *cough* The Electric Slide *cough* And here is where the customization comes in...   I can provide you with a combination of live music and “DJ” services so there is something for everyone!
